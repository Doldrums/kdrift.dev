§ 02 — FAQ
Frequently asked questions
What counts as a migration run?
Each invocation of kdrift migrate for a single driver across a kernel version range. Check and validate don't count.
Can I use KDRIFT with vendor kernels?
Team plan supports standard vendor kernels (Android common, Ubuntu, RHEL). Enterprise supports fully custom forks with divergent APIs.
What if a migration fails?
KDRIFT never delivers an unvalidated patch. Failed migrations are logged with diagnostics. Enterprise gets root-cause analysis.
Do you store our driver source code?
No. KDRIFT runs locally or in your CI. Source code never leaves your infrastructure. Enterprise self-hosted option available.
Can I switch plans?
Yes, upgrade or downgrade anytime. Team to Enterprise migration is seamless.
What LLM models are used?
KDRIFT supports multiple backends: Claude, GPT, Qwen, DeepSeek. You choose the model. Enterprise can use KERN fine-tuned models.
